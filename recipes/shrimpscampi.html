Spicy Cajun Shrimp Scampi

A plate that tastes great and easy to make after a long day of work! Your friends and family will rave about your cooking ability after tasting this easy but delicious shrimp scampi!

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Angel Hair Pasta
  • Cajun Seasoning
  • Lemon Wedges
  • Butter
  • Diced Garlic

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a saute pan over medium heat
  2. Dice garlic and add to cajun seasoning in a mixing bowl
  3. Coat shrimp in the seasoning then brown in saute pan with butter
  4. Bring a sauce pan with water to a boil
  5. Add angel hair pasta to the boiling water
  6. Strain pasta and add to the sauce pan with browned shrimp
  7. Simmer for 2-3 minutes, squeeze garlic wedges, serve!
