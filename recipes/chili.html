A Warm Pot of Chili for a Cold Winter Night

This is the best bowl of chili you can find east of the mississippi! All of the ingredients are farm fresh and of the highest quality. Rest assured you have found the recipe for chili in the world.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Pinto Beans
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Chili Powder
  • Cayenne Powder
  • Kidney Beans
  • Chopped Onions
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Place the ground beef in a large Pot
  2. throw in garlic
  3. Cook over medium heat until browned
  4. Drain off excess fat - pour in tomato sauce
  5. Add seasonings, stir well then simmer for 1 hour while stirring occasionally
  6. Serve hot with cheddar cheese
