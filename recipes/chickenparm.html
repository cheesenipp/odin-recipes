A Hot Plate of Cheesy Chicken Parm

This chicken parm recipe is as cheesy as it is heavenly. The perfect homemade sauce and the best amish cheese churned on the farm!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Egg yolk

Steps

  1. Bread the chicken breasts by first dipping them in egg yolk
  2. Next place the chicken breasts into bread crumbs
  3. Heat a pan on medium heat with vegetable oil
  4. Place breaded chicken into the hot pan and cook until the coating is golden brown
  5. Heat marinara sauce in a sauce pan while chicken is browning
  6. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  7. Place browned chicken on a lined sheet pan and top with Mozzarella and Parmesan cheese
  8. Bake for 20 mins or until internal temperature reaches 165 degrees fahrenheit
  9. Place chicken on a plate, pour over marinara sauce, and top with more parmesan cheese
